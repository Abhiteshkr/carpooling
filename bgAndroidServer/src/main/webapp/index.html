Serveur Architecture

I need to manage a lot of client (Cars and Pedestrian) And i have not so much CPU.
So i choosed to be the lightest i could.
I choose a tomcat server, directly in front of clients. No database. The data of clients are keep the minimum of time. Android client refresh its position every n minutes.So ,data are deleted after 3*n minutes of inactivity. It is not only useless to send inactive data to a client, but it is confusing.
I tested i can have more than 10000 users in the same time. It is enough to start!
