Carpool Presentation

Car pooling:travel, save money and help the planet!

With Android, carpooling will be easier:
  • location using GPS
  • communication using Google Talk
  • visualization using Google Map
And all inside one single sexy java application!
Carpooling with Android assists both pedestrians and drivers to locate, and contact each other, increasing travel options, decreasing travel costs, and opening an accessible carpooling system to general users.
As a real time system, Carpooling with Android could be a future mass transportation system for the general public, reducing traffic congestion and enabling every car to act as a taxi.
With Google's assistance, Android will change the concept of people transportation and will be of great benefit in both developing and developed countries.
